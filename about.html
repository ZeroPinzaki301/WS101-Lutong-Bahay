Meet the members of Group 7, the team behind the Lutong Bahay website. We are a group of students from the Baliwag Polytehnic College who made this website as our project for Web Systems and Technologies under the supervision of Prof. Regina Santos

Project Manager



Leads the team with strategic planning and organization, ensuring that all phases of the project align with the intended objectives. Through detailed project documentation, essential information is recorded for seamless execution. Quality assurance measures are strictly implemented to maintain consistency and reliability. Timeline management is a top priority, ensuring that deadlines are met efficiently without compromising the quality of work. Beyond management, contributions also extend to UI layout assistance, where careful attention is given to achieving the perfect balance between functionality and aesthetics.
